## Designing responsible AI for enterprise

Deploying a frontier model in a regulated company raises questions that don't exist for individual use: where does data reside, who can access what, how is usage audited, and what happens when a model is updated? Our view is that answering those questions well and upfront is part of deploying AI responsibly.

We built Claude Enterprise around a set of commitments we think any organization should expect from a frontier AI provider. Customer prompts and responses aren't used to train our models by default. Retention is configurable. Administrators get the controls they need to govern access without standing between employees and the work.

### How is Claude Enterprise different from individual or team plans?

Claude Enterprise is built for workforce-wide deployment under central governance. The product itself is the same Claude, but it adds the identity management, data controls, audit infrastructure, and contractual structure that enterprise IT, security, and procurement teams require. Individual and team plans don't carry that infrastructure because individual and team use don't require it.

### Does Anthropic use Claude Enterprise internally?

Yes. Every Anthropic employee, across every function, works on Claude Enterprise under the same governance model we offer customers. Researchers use it for safety and interpretability work. Legal, finance, recruiting, and operations use it for the work that fills their week. The product you'd evaluate is the one we rely on every day.

### Are customer prompts or responses used to train Anthropic's models?

No, not by default. Customer data on Claude Enterprise is not used to train our models. Retention is configurable, and the specifics of our data handling are published in our Trust Center.

### What does "frontier model" mean, and why does it matter for enterprise deployment?

A frontier model is one operating at the leading edge of capability. Deploying a frontier model across a workforce raises governance and safety questions that don't exist for narrower or more limited models, which is why Claude Enterprise is built around the controls and commitments outlined on this page.

### Can Claude Enterprise be deployed alongside Claude Code and Claude Cowork?

Yes. Claude Enterprise covers Claude Code and Claude Cowork under a single agreement, so engineering and business teams can deploy together without fragmenting procurement or security review across products.

### Where can I find Anthropic's security and compliance documentation?

Anthropic's Trust Center publishes our certifications, sub-processors, data handling policies, and security commitments. It's the authoritative source for enterprise security and compliance reviewers.
